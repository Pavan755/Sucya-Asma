# Narration Script (Draft 1)

Can even the smallest effort become part of something extraordinary?

According to a cherished tradition associated with the Ramayana, this is the story of a tiny squirrel whose devotion touched Lord Rama himself.

As Rama's army worked tirelessly to build the great bridge across the ocean to Lanka, mighty vanaras carried enormous stones and boulders.

Amidst this monumental effort, a small squirrel wished to help.

Unable to lift massive rocks, it rolled in the sand and gently shook the grains into the spaces between the larger stones.

To many, its contribution seemed insignificant.

But Rama noticed something others had overlooked.

He saw sincerity.

He saw devotion.

He saw a heart that wished to serve with whatever it could offer.

Moved by the squirrel's dedication, Rama gently stroked its back in blessing.

A beloved tradition says that the stripes seen on Indian palm squirrels today are reminders of that divine touch.

Whether great or small, every sincere effort carries its own value.

Sometimes, it is not the size of our contribution that matters most, but the intention behind it.
