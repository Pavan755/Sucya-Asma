# Story Verification: The Squirrel and Rama's Bridge

## Story Status

Verified for adaptation.

---

## Story Summary

A small squirrel contributes to the construction of Rama Setu by carrying grains of sand to fill gaps between larger stones placed by the vanaras. Lord Rama recognizes its devotion and blesses it. Tradition associates the stripes on Indian palm squirrels with Rama's touch.

---

## Primary Source Classification

This episode is not explicitly found in the commonly accepted versions of the Valmiki Ramayana.
