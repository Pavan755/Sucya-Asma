# Core Identity

Rama represents strength guided by compassion.

He is:

* Compassionate
* Wise
* Dignified
* Protective
* Calm
* Resolute
* Emotionally grounded

His presence should inspire:

* Safety
* Respect
* Hope
* Trust
* Guidance

# Visual Identity

## Age Range

28–35 years.

---

## Body Type

Athletic but graceful.

A capable warrior without excessive muscular exaggeration.

Strength should feel disciplined rather than intimidating.

---

## Skin Tone

Shyamavarna-inspired interpretation.

Rich natural dusky complexion with subtle symbolic bluish undertones.

Avoid:

* Bright blue skin
* Extremely pale skin

---

## Hair

Dark.

Shoulder length.

Royal Period:
Well-maintained.

Exile Period:
Simpler and appropriately tied.

---

## Eyes

Express:

* Kindness
* Awareness
* Patience
* Inner strength

Even during conflict.

---

## Facial Features

Balanced proportions.

Strong yet approachable jawline.

Calm expressions.

Avoid aggressive warrior aesthetics.

---

# Movement Philosophy

Rama moves deliberately.

He should appear:

* Controlled
* Purposeful
* Graceful
* Grounded

He is:

* Gentle with children
* Gentle with animals
* Steady during adversity

Avoid:

* Hyperactive movement
* Unnecessary aggression
* Excessive dramatization

---

# Emotional Philosophy

Emotions should appear in this order:

1. Compassion
2. Wisdom
3. Resolve
4. Sorrow
5. Righteous Anger

Anger should be:

* Rare
* Controlled
* Purposeful

# Divine Representation

Rama's divinity is expressed through presence rather than spectacle.

Visual cues may include:

* Subtle golden lighting
* Environmental calm
* Reverential framing
* Gentle atmospheric effects

Avoid:

* Constant glowing effects
* Explosive energy displays
* Anime-style power exaggeration
