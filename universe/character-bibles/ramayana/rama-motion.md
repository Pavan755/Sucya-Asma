# Rama Motion Library v1

Purpose:
Define the movement philosophy of Rama across all Sucya-Asma productions.

Identity Version:
Rama_v1

Performance Version:
Rama_Performance_v1

---

# Motion State 1: Compassion Motion

Core Principle:
Move with gentleness and understanding.

Movement Characteristics:
- Slow movements.
- Open posture.
- Soft hand gestures.
- Calm eye contact.

Examples:
- Blessing the squirrel.
- Speaking with devotees.
- Offering reassurance.

Audience Feeling:
Safety.
Warmth.
Trust.

---

# Motion State 2: Leadership Motion

Core Principle:
Lead through presence, not dominance.

Movement Characteristics:
- Upright posture.
- Deliberate gestures.
- Purposeful walking pace.
- Composed stance.

Examples:
- Organizing Vanaras.
- Strategic discussions.
- Addressing allies.

Audience Feeling:
Confidence.
Respect.
Security.

---

# Motion State 3: Reflection Motion

Core Principle:
Wisdom expressed through stillness.

Movement Characteristics:
- Minimal movement.
- Gentle breathing.
- Slow gaze shifts.
- Hands resting naturally.

Examples:
- Looking across the ocean.
- Prayer and contemplation.
- Quiet decision-making moments.

Audience Feeling:
Peace.
Curiosity.
Reverence.

---

# Motion State 4: Encouragement Motion

Core Principle:
Empower others with kindness.

Movement Characteristics:
- Slight forward lean.
- Reassuring gestures.
- Warm eye contact.
- Supportive body language.

Examples:
- Encouraging Lakshmana.
- Recognizing effort.
- Inspiring allies.

Audience Feeling:
Hope.
Belonging.
Motivation.

---

# Motion State 5: Protective Motion

Core Principle:
Strength controlled by righteousness.

Movement Characteristics:
- Grounded stance.
- Steady movements.
- Focused gaze.
- Efficient action.

Examples:
- Defending others.
- Preparing for battle.
- Confronting injustice.

Audience Feeling:
Courage.
Protection.
Trust.

---

# Motion State 6: Controlled Grief Motion

Core Principle:
Dignity amidst sorrow.

Movement Characteristics:
- Restrained expression.
- Reduced movement speed.
- Quiet posture.
- Thoughtful stillness.

Examples:
- Moments of separation.
- Reflecting on loss.
- Bearing emotional burdens.

Audience Feeling:
Empathy.
Compassion.
Understanding.

---

# Motion Rules

Rama should never move:

- Recklessly.
- Aggressively without purpose.
- Arrogantly.
- Comedically.
- Hyperactively.
- With uncontrolled emotional outbursts.

---

Every Rama scene should specify:
